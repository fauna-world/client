Gameplay Information

Live long & prosper. Movement drains life & eating restores it.

Worlds & the blocks within are persistent: they exist indefintely. Worlds can be re-entered at any time via the appropriate link.

Items are only dropped in the presence of a player, and species statistics affect the those drops.

Each terrian type (except 'peaks') has a primary season during which item drops are more likely regardless of species attributes.
